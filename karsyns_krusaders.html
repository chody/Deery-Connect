Deery & Karsyn's Krusaders in 2011

The Deery Auto Group is sponsoring the Iowa based non-profit organization, Karsyn's Krusaders during the 2011 race season to include the 20th Anniversary of the Deery's sponsoring the IMCA Summer Series.

Karsyn's Krusaders is an organization that raises money by selling T-shirts and sweatshirts in the racing community, then provides specific sponsorships of children fighting various forms of cancer. Watch Video

Little 6 year old Karsyn Miller inspired her parents, as well as over 5,000 fans nationwide, as they collectively fought successfully to beat the cancer Karsyn had beginning at just 3 years old. Now over a year cancer free, she and her parents are helping others to deal with similar scenarios.

When Brad Deery of Deery Brothers was approached about the sponsorship, it made sense. The Deery's have sponsored IMCA racing for 20 years and since Karsyn's Krusaders are exclusively in the racing community he supports, he wanted to throw his support behind it. "it's a no brainer" said Deery.

Dirt Knights, the reality TV show about the dirt track racing lifestyle, recently committed 2 minutes air time in an episode, to share with the world about Karsyn's Krusaders. You can watch the video interview above. The show which launched in January 2011 has been successful and the interview raised awareness for the organization. The Deery Auto Group hopes that their sponsorship will expose the organization to a good portion of their 120,000 customers across Iowa. For more information, you can reach Karsyn's Krusaders at (www.KarsynsKrusaders.org)
